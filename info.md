1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
   Thriving Youth Pathways is a youth development NGO serving boys and girls aged 8–18 through
   structured mental health awareness programs, character formation, and practical life‑skills training in
   etiquette, homemaking, craftsmanship, cuisine, and entrepreneurship.
   The initiative responds to growing youth mental health challenges and declining practical life
   competence by providing integrated education, skill‑building, and mentorship that strengthen
   emotional wellbeing, responsible behavior, and independent living readiness.
   We seek funding to implement a 12‑month Mental Health & Life Skills Youth Development Program
   combining workshops, practical skill labs, mentorship, and youth enterprise projects.
2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
   Across many communities, young people face:
   Rising anxiety, stress, and low self‑esteem
   Limited emotional coping and mental health literacy
   Weak practical life and independent living skills
   Declining social etiquette and respectful conduct
   Limited access to structured after‑school developmental programs
   Minimal exposure to entrepreneurship and financial responsibility
   Without early support, youth may enter adulthood lacking emotional resilience, self‑management skills,
   and confidence in everyday life tasks such as cooking, home care, communication, and
   decision‑making.
   There is a critical need for holistic youth programs that integrate mental health awareness, character
   formation, and practical life readiness.
